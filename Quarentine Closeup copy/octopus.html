Crochet Octopus

After learning how to hand crochet, I decided to get hooks and try making a stuffed animal. This is the first plush I made and his name is Oswald. I am very proud of how this turned out for my first one, and it definetly influenced me to crochet a lot more. Looking back at this now, this is definetly not my favorite thing I have ever made, but it does make me realize and appreciate how much I have grown my skills and it will always be one of my favorite hobbies!
